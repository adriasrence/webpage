Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our coffee shop

General Questions

What are your business hours? +
We are open Monday to Friday from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Saturday from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and Sunday from 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM.
Do you offer WiFi? +
Yes, we provide free high-speed WiFi for all our customers. The password is available at the counter.
Is there parking available? +
Yes, we have a small parking lot behind the shop, and street parking is also available nearby.

Menu & Orders

Do you offer dairy-free alternatives? +
Yes, we offer almond milk, oat milk, and soy milk as alternatives to regular milk.
Can I place an order for pickup? +
Yes, you can place orders for pickup through our website or by calling the store directly.
Do you have gluten-free options? +
Yes, we offer several gluten-free pastries and snacks. Please ask our staff for recommendations.

Events & Catering

Do you offer catering services? +
Yes, we provide catering services for events. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to discuss your needs.
Can I book the space for private events? +
Yes, we offer space rental for private events after business hours. Please contact us for availability and pricing.
