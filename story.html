Our Journey

A tale of passion, dedication, and exceptional coffee

The Beginning

Founded in 2010, Coffee Aroma began as a small passion project between two coffee enthusiasts who shared a dream of creating a space where people could experience exceptional coffee in a warm, welcoming environment. What started as a tiny corner shop has grown into a beloved community hub.

2010

Opened our first location in Brooklyn

2013

Launched our barista training program

2015

Started sourcing beans directly from farmers

2018

Expanded to three locations

Our Philosophy

At Coffee Aroma, we believe that every cup of coffee tells a story. From the carefully selected beans sourced from sustainable farms to our expert baristas who craft each drink with precision and care, we're committed to delivering an unforgettable coffee experience. Our dedication to quality and sustainability drives everything we do.

Our Team

Our team of passionate baristas undergoes rigorous training to master the art of coffee making. We take pride in our staff's expertise and their ability to create the perfect cup of coffee while providing exceptional customer service. Each team member brings their unique personality and skills to create a welcoming atmosphere for our guests.

Community Impact

Beyond serving great coffee, we're committed to making a positive impact in our community. Through partnerships with local suppliers and various community initiatives, we strive to create a sustainable and inclusive coffee culture. We regularly host events, workshops, and support local artists and musicians.
